## Installation
Extract ``lan_of_doom_remove_objectives.tar.gz`` to your server's ``css/cstrike``
directory.

## Console Variables
``sm_lanofdoom_remove_objectives`` If ``1``, objectives are removed at round
start. ``1`` by default.
